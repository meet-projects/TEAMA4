About Mydea

Mydea is about sharing mydeas that would inspire community leaders and entrepreneurs to take action and solve social issues. You can share a mydea about an issue in your community. If your mydea will gain a lot of supporters, there is a greater chance that your community leader would see and solve your common issue. You can also share a mydea about a common everyday problem. If your mydea will gain a lot of supporters, there is a greater chance that your mydea would inspire entrepreneurs to make a product that can solve your problem.
